In the old French town along Suffren Street, Villa Shanti is just a 3-minute walk from the famous Promenade Beach. It provides a 24-hour front desk and free Wi-Fi access throughout the entire property.

Decorated with wood furnishings, including a wardrobe and a sitting area, these spacious air-conditioned rooms include a personal safe, a satellite TV and a minibar. The private bathrooms have shower facilities.

At Villa Shanti, guests can request luggage storage, as well as laundry and ironing services. The tour desk can assist with travel bookings. An airport shuttle service and local shuttle services can be arranged for an additional fee. Guests can also rent a car to explore the area, with the convenience of free parking located nearby.
